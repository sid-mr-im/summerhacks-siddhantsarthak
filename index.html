About

What? Why? How?

  • What are We?

    We’re a community of innovators, engineers, researchers and tech enthusiasts who have come together to ideate, design, prototype and create products which are novel, with a spark of sustainable future. We are a place where great ideas become even better products.

  • Why we're doing this?

    Our vision is to create a Sustainable Future. One which thrives fabricating technologies - with a less cost of nature as possible. Most of our projects are aligned with the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals.

  • How do we do it?

    We encourage everyone to participate in the dissemination of ideas to design and create things. Together we design, innovate and create, such that you have a better chance in making bigger positive impact on our world.

  • Expansion

Smorption

-Siddhant & Uditanshu

Several attempts are being made worldwide on personal, industrial and governmental levels to curb the intensity at which air pollution is rising and regain a balance as far as the proportions of the foundation gases are concerned. This is a direct attempt at slacking Global warming. We are seeing a series of innovations and experiments aimed at alternate and unconventional options to reduce pollutants. Air Pollution is one of the larger mirrors of man’s follies, and a challenge we need to overcome to see a tomorrow.

  • Offered Incubation by STPI, Bhubaneswar

Gredits

-Siddhant, Ayush, Vinayak & Suraj

Many people contribute in carbon footprint throwing ecological balance off scale. So after much discussion our team agreed on choosing. Optimization of carbon footprint by each individual and assign credits to limit the scope of unsustainable activities. If the governments across the globe are serious to take a step in reducing their air pollution index, why not start with its citizens? So, we thought of a mobile app: Gredits which is Green+Credits. You go green, you get rewarded.

  • Runners Up YfS Green Hackathon
  • Incubated in IdeaNest, AIC

Cardttendance

-Siddhant, Ayush, Vinayak & Sai

Manual time and attendance management systems totally rely on humans and humans are not perfect. With manual systems, it is a great pressure on management to be correct in all details work at all times. So we revamped the system using IoT and RFID.

  • Runners Up in "31 Days Project Challenge"
